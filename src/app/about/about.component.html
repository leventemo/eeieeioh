eeieeioh

This site is meant to help student and teacher do their own thing with English.

Organizational, national, international, continental, global – and probably universal – shortage of paper, combined with an abundance of mobile phones in our hands is nudging us to put it all online. On top of that, it's 2022.

inspiration

The word inspiration has very nice connotations but the idea here is very simple and practical: whenever there is a need for student or teacher to have quick and easy access to any type of prompt in class or at home, we try to make it available online. The truth is, we have much more ideas than we can cope with in terms of development and content writing but we like it this way. Better to have ideas you can't make happen just now than the other way round.

plans

Lots of. We hope to see the site grow and become a full-blown learning platform one day. We are working on our own digital versions of popular, well-tried activities and games and we're also experimenting with new ideas.

target audience

Although in order to achieve successful learning, teachers need students, students don't always need teachers. We're trying to make the life of both groups easier and that means the tools and content you find here should be designed to be used in and/or outside of the classroom.

activities

We're working on a variety of activities; they're going to be the backbone of eeieeioh. Each of them are designed for students to do different things with English. They each require a specific "setup" of how many learners do them (learners on their own, in pairs, small groups or as a whole class), who has a phone with the prompts and how they use them, is there a need for a projector, etc. When opening an activity, click on theinfo_outline icon for instructions.

Cards

The simplest activity of all: students press a button, get some prompt, then do something.

Duel

Not unlike Cards but it has timers and counters for 2 players who rush to chose between a correct and an incorrect form of a word, a phrase or anything really. Designed for quick and somewhat personalized error correction. Coming soon.

tools

Marksheets

A tool for mock exams that enables teachers to record results and get insights into how individual students and groups are making progress and what needs to be worked on. Coming soon.
